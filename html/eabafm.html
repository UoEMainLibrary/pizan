Encore Aultres Balades

Harley MS 4431 contains the only known copy of Encore Aultres Balades; the name is misleading for these 9 poems consist of 4 rondeaux quatrains, 4 balades simples and 1 balade layée.

Although the collection has no rubric, the four lines left blank before the first poem, together with two faint dots closeby in the outer margin, show that one was planned. The rubric supplied in this edition mirrors the only running title, at the top of fol. 049v, and echoes the title found in the Table of Contents.

The collection was inserted into the Queen's MS at the very last moment, witness the very unusual structure of Quire 5 and the allusions in the final poem to the expedition led by the Duke of Bourbon which culminated in the capture of Soubise on 23 November 1413.
