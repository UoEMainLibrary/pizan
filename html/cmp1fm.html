Une Autre Complainte Amoureuse

The Complainte is introduced by a miniature and a rubric. There is a complete set of running titles, save that the title on fol. 57r has been almost entirely cropped by the binder.

The poem is made up of 15 seizains, rhyming a10a10a10b4b10b10b10c4c10c10c10d4d10d10d10e4e10e10e10f4 etc. The metrical structure is identical to that of the other two Complaintes in the Queen's MS: Item 10 (Une Complainte Amoureuse), and the conclusion of Item 18 (Le Livre du Duc des Vrais Amans).

The opening line of Stanza 1 is set off by a champie initial and a border. The plan that each of the other stanzas should be introduced by a paragraph mark was not fully implemented. Only six were executed, before Stanzas 7 and 11-15; the marginal instructions left before Stanzas 2-6 and 8-10 were overlooked.

There is no obvious reason for the one blank line, which is found halfway through Stanza 1. With that exception, the stanzas are copied without a break. In this edition a blank line has been left after each stanza, mirroring the layout of the other two Complaintes.
