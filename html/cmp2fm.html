Complainte Amoureuse

Harley MS 4431 contains the only known copy of the Complainte Amoureuse. The unusual structure of Quire 5 shows that the Complainte, together with Encore Aultres Balades, the item immediately following, was added to the Queen's MS at the very last minute.

The Complainte is introduced by a miniature and a rubric; there is only one running title, on fol. 49r. The poem is made up of 12 seizains, rhyming a10a10a10b4b10b10b10c4c10c10c10d4d10d10d10e4e10e10e10f4 etc. The opening line of each stanza is set off: by a champie initial with a border (Stanza 1) or by a paragraph mark (Stanzas 2-12); unusually, Stanza 9 contains a second paragraph mark, at line 135. A blank line has been left after each stanza, except after Stanzas 2 and 11. Lines 149-51 have been copied twice; the second version is scored through by a single vertical line.
