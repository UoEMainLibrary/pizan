Ph.D. Project Description

My Ph.D research aims at developing a new method of distinguishing between scribal hands in medieval manuscripts. Elaborating on the idea of a scribal fingerprint – a unique set of thirteen criteria present in each hand (as described in my MPhil Thesis) – I intend to answer the following questions:

(1) Is it possible, using existing computer-controlled handwriting analysis programmes, to identify the scribal fingerprint?
          (1.1) Is it possible to objectively distinguish between scribal hands by using the scribal fingerprint method?

(2) When applying the concept of the scribal fingerprint to the original manuscripts of works of Christine de Pizan, what can we say about the autographic character of the original manuscripts and Christine’s contribution to their making?
